⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTES & PRECAUTIONS

Critical Points to Remember:

1. Property Sale Before 23rd July 2024: Since your property was acquired before this date, you can choose between 12.5% (without indexation) or 20% (with indexation). Properties acquired after this date do not get indexation benefit.

2. Section 54 Restrictions:
   • New property must be residential only (not commercial)
   • Cannot sell the new property for 3 years (will become taxable)
   • Can buy maximum 2 properties only once in lifetime under Section 54
   • Must not own more than one residential property (other than new property) on date of new purchase

3. Section 54EC Restrictions:
   • Cannot withdraw/pledge bonds for 5 years
   • If withdrawn early, the exemption claimed will be reversed
   • Interest earned on bonds is fully taxable
   • Maximum limit is Rs 50 lakhs per financial year

4. CGAS Account:
   • Must be opened before filing ITR
   • Protects your exemption claim even if investment is delayed
   • Withdrawal only for specified purposes
   • Unused amount after timeline becomes taxable

5. Advance Tax: Since capital gains exceed Rs 10,000, you must pay advance tax. Interest under Section 234B and 234C may apply for non-payment.

6. Both Regimes Same for LTCG: The capital gains tax rate is same in both old and new tax regimes. Choose regime based on your total income and other deductions available.

7. Senior Citizen Benefits: Higher basic exemption limit and higher 80D deduction (Rs 50,000) in old regime.
